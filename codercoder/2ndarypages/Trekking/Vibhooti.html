About the place

The word "Vibhuthi" means limestone. The water in this falls comes past limestone rocks near Yana and so the falls is called as "Vibhuthi Falls".[1] Nearest towns near Vibhuthi falls are Sirsi , Kumta, Ankola, Gokarna.
