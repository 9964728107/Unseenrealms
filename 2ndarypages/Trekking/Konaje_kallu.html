About the place

Konaje is a village in Dakshina Kannada District of Karnataka. The place is 7km away from the city, Moodbidri in the Naravi Road. KonajeKallu is a large granite standing stone. It is a challenge for rock climbers since it has no footholds and will be a trekkers paradise. It is also a place of worship for the believers and paradise for trekkers. There is Kalabairava and MahamayiGudi near KonajeKallu. The distance between one gigantic granite block to another is a half kilometer. From far, it looks like a monolithic rock. There is a bond between the two granite blocks, which is called ‘Boodikere,’ where one gets pure water throughout the year.
