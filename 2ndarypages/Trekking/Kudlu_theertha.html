About the place

The waterfall is also called Sita Falls which is known for its beauty.This waterfall flows down towards the Western Ghats from a height of 150 feet amidst dense forest.The waterfall is located about 24kms from Udupi town and can be reached by road.To reach Kudlu falls visitors need to trek through the forest for about 4kms.The trek is relatively easy and takes about 1-2 hours to complete.The waterfall cascades down down from a height of about 150 feet,creating a beautiful pool at the bottom.Visitors can take also enjoy other activities like camping,trekking and birdwatching in the area.The best time to visit the waterfall is during the monsoon season,from June to September.
