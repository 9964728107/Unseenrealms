About the place

it’s a third highest peak in coorg & 2nd highest in Karnataka located at an elevation 1748m the gentle slopes & grassy terrain lure you with scenic charm & serene ambience .there is nakaland palace &a Padi Igguthappa temple which was built in late 1970’s as a safe house for kodagu rulers chikka veera rajendra who was being targeted by the british .
